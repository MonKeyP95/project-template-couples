# DESIGN.md

## Overall Style
Warm, soft, modern, and collaborative. The app should feel calming and loving rather than corporate or cold.

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Soft pink (#f8c1d4) or warm peach
- **Accent**: Gentle teal (#7dd3fc) or sage green
- **Background**: Warm off-white (#faf7f5) or light beige
- **Text**: Deep warm gray (#374151)
- **Success**: Soft green
- **Warning**: Soft orange

## Design Principles
- Mobile-first design
- Plenty of whitespace (avoid feeling cluttered)
- Both partners should feel it belongs to "us"
- Soft shadows and rounded corners
- Consistent icon style
- Calm and welcoming atmosphere

## Typography
- Clean sans-serif fonts (Inter or similar)
- Clear hierarchy with good contrast
